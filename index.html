About

I'm a UI Designer who builds at the intersection of craft, systems, and responsibility. My work lives in design systems — the tokens, components, and documentation that power products used by real people. I care deeply about accessibility not as a compliance checkbox but as a design principle, and I believe every decision we make — from contrast ratios to payload size — has a footprint.

  • Based in Mechelen — open to remote work in Europe and Hybrid in Belgium
  • Available for new work
  • Design systems, component design, atomic design approach, & clean design
  • WCAG 2.1/2.2 practitioner
  • Sustainable & performance-first design
